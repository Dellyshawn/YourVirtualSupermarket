About Us


We have developed a platform to address the challenges Jamaican consumers face in accessing the benefits of large-scale supermarkets.
The Virtual Supermarket creates growth opportunities for smaller-scale supermarkets and ensures that all customers can enjoy the advantages
of remote shopping. By extending discounts, membership perks, and convenient shopping options, we enable individuals with disabilities, visual
impairments, medical conditions, or difficulties traveling to urban areas to access these benefits. Join us in revolutionizing the shopping
experience and promoting inclusivity, where everyone can enjoy the convenience of remote shopping.
